Who are we?

We are three students: Jamal Tawfiq Fathy Tawfiq, Mariam Ali Mohamed Al-Shahawy, and Ruba Mohamed Saleem Al-Baba, studying under the supervision of Dr. Iman Abu Marya. We are working on designing a simple and user-friendly website aimed at providing high-quality skincare and haircare products, along with great offers that cater to customer needs. Our goal is to enhance the online shopping experience by offering a comfortable user interface and attractive design that allows customers to explore products easily. We aspire for this site to be a reference for everything related to skincare and haircare, providing useful tips and articles to raise awareness of the best care methods. We are committed to making this project a practical example reflecting our skills and delivering valuable experiences to users.

The Idea

The website idea revolves around creating an integrated and easy-to-use platform dedicated to providing high-quality skincare and haircare products. The site aims to provide a smooth shopping experience for customers, combining easy navigation and finding the required products, with clear and helpful information about each product. Through this site, we also aim to raise awareness of the importance of personal care by offering specialized tips and guidance, as well as exclusive offers and special packages. Our vision is for the site to become a trusted destination for customers seeking excellence and quality in self-care products.
