Velve - Frequently Asked Questions

Who are we?
We are three students: Jamal Tawfiq Fathy Tawfiq, Mariam Ali Mohamed Al-Shahawy, and Ruba Mohamed Saleem Al-Baba, working under the supervision of Dr. Iman Abu Marya. Our mission is to provide high-quality skincare and haircare products through a simple and user-friendly website.
What is the idea of this website?
The website is an integrated platform dedicated to high-quality skincare and haircare products. It combines ease of use with helpful product information to enhance the shopping experience.
What kind of products do you offer?
We offer natural moisturizing creams, hair oils, chemical-free shampoos, anti-aging serums, and nourishing masks made from organic ingredients suitable for all skin and hair types.
What are the shipping times?
Shipping times vary by location but generally range from 1 to 2 days within the region.
How can I contact customer support?
You can reach us via the "Contact Us" page or email us at support@velve.com. Our team is available daily from 8 AM to 3 PM.
Are your products safe for sensitive skin?
Yes, our products are made from natural and organic ingredients, making them safe and suitable for sensitive skin types.
Do you offer international shipping?
Currently, we ship within the region, but we are working on expanding our services to international locations soon.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit/debit cards, online payment platforms, and cash on delivery for your convenience.
